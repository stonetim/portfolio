Celebrating yoU

Homecoming 2016 will be a celebration of the greatest part of Evangel University…you! Mark your calendars for
October 20-22, 2016. The main events will be held on Thursday, Friday and Saturday, so join with fellow alumni to reconnect and celebrate. Graduation classes from 1966, 1976, 1991 and 2006 will enjoy a special reunion dinner surrounded with other Friday events like Kids U-Night and the Friday Festival on the Quad.
See the full schedule below and register today for Homecoming 2016!
