Celebrating yoU

Homecoming 2015 is a celebration of the greatest part of Evangel University…you! Join your friends and family Oct. 1-3 for a number of favorite events and the return of EU Soccer. Think of the moments you celebrate in life…a promotion at work, a birthday with friends, or the simple joy of engaging in a life that impacts different corners of the world. Celebrate your impact and the place that helped make it possible: Evangel University. Celebrating yoU is your chance to catch up with friends and greet others that call Evangel home.
