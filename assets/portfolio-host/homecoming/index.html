Homecoming 2017 will be a celebration of the greatest part of Evangel University…you! Mark your calendars for October 5-7, 2017. The main events will be held on Thursday, Friday and Saturday, so join with fellow alumni to reconnect and celebrate. Graduation classes from 1959-1965 (Pioneers), 1967, 1977, 1987, 1992, 1997 and 2007 will enjoy a special reunion dinner surrounded with other Friday events like Kids U-Night and the Friday Festival on the Quad.
See the full schedule below or download a PDF schedule here for Homecoming 2017!
